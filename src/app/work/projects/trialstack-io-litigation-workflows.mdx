## Overview

TrialStack.io standardizes repeatable workflows—intake routing, segmented campaigns, member onboarding, and reporting—into configurable automations.

## My role
Product strategy, platform architecture, GTM enablement

## Stack
Next.js, TypeScript, Supabase • n8n, Make, OpenAI (agents) • Salesforce Marketing Cloud, HubSpot

## Highlights
- Cut manual ops time ~40% by replacing handoffs with async automations.
- Unified CRM/marketing/CMS data flows with resilient retries and idempotency.
- Templated operational playbooks for 30+ associations and events.

## Outcome
An automation-first product powering legal ops and marketing with measurable time savings and clearer attribution.
